## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the app at [meal-planner-umber-nu.vercel.app](https://meal-planner-umber-nu.vercel.app)
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile setup

### First-Time Setup

After creating your account, you'll be guided through:
- Basic information (name, age, gender)
- Current weight and target weight
- Health goals selection
- Dietary preferences
- Any medical conditions or allergies

## Meal Planning

### How to Plan Your Meals

1. Go to **Meals** from the dashboard
2. Select the day you want to plan
3. Click on a meal slot (breakfast, lunch, etc.)
4. Browse or search for meals
5. Click **Add to Plan**

### Meal Information

Each meal shows:
- **Calories**: Energy content
- **Macros**: Protein, carbs, fats
- **Benefits**: Health benefits
- **Prep Time**: How long to prepare
- **Ingredients**: What you need
- **Recipe**: Step-by-step instructions

### Finding Meals

- **Search**: Type a meal name
- **Filter by Cuisine**: Italian, Ugandan, Indian, etc.
- **Filter by Type**: Breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack
- **Easy Prep**: Meals under 15 minutes
- **Raw/Fresh**: No-cook options

### East African Dishes

We include traditional dishes from:
- **Uganda**: Matooke, Rolex, Luwombo, Katogo
- **Kenya**: Ugali, Nyama Choma, Sukuma Wiki
- **Tanzania**: Pilau, Mishkaki, Chipsi Mayai
- **Rwanda**: Isombe, Brochettes, Ubugali
- **Burundi**: Beans & Plantains, Mukeke

## Tracking Your Progress

### Daily Logging

Log these daily:
1. **Meals Eaten**: Mark meals as complete
2. **Weight**: Optional daily weigh-in

4. **Mood**: How you're feeling

### Viewing Reports

- **Daily**: Today's summary

- **Weight Graph**: Track weight over time
- **Calorie Trends**: Daily intake patterns

## Reminders

### Setting Up Reminders

1. Go to **Reminders**
2. Click **Add Reminder**
3. Choose type:
   - Meal reminder
   - Weight check
   - Water reminder
   - Goal check-in
4. Set the time
5. Select days of the week
6. Save

### Missed Meal Alerts

If you miss a meal:
- You'll receive a gentle reminder
- Get suggestions to make up for it
- Option to log it late or skip

## Rewards & Motivation

### How Rewards Work

Earn rewards by:
- Completing daily meals
- Maintaining streaks
- Reaching milestones
- Logging consistently

### Types of Rewards

| Reward | How to Earn |
|--------|-------------|
| Daily Treat | Complete all meals |
| Streak Badge | 3, 7, 14, 30 day streaks |
| Milestone | Reach weight goals |
| Off Day | Earned through consistency |

### Off Days

- Earn off days through good streaks
- Use them guilt-free
- No calorie tracking required
- Enjoy your favorite foods!

## Exercise & Workouts

### Tracking Workouts

1. Go to **Exercise**
2. Click **Log Workout**
3. Select workout type:
   - **Home**: Bodyweight, yoga, stretching
   - **Gym**: Equipment-based exercises
4. Enter duration
5. Save and earn tokens!

### Workout Tokens

- Earn tokens for each workout
- Redeem for rewards
- Track your exercise streak
- See calories burned

### Goal-Aligned Workouts

Workouts are suggested based on your goal:
- **Weight Loss**: Cardio, HIIT
- **Maintenance**: Mixed routines
- **Muscle Gain**: Strength training

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! Your data is encrypted and never shared without your consent.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Basic features work offline. Data syncs when you're back online.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: The web app works like a mobile app! Add it to your home screen.

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Change Password

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account

### Meal Questions

**Q: Can I add my own meals?**
A: Yes! Go to Meals > Add Custom Meal

**Q: How accurate are the calories?**
A: We use verified nutrition databases. Home-cooked meals are estimates.

### Technical Questions

**Q: The app isn't loading?**
A: Try refreshing, clearing cache, or using a different browser.

**Q: Reminders aren't working?**
A: Check your browser notification settings and allow notifications.
